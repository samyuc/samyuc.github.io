--- layout: default --- Hi! I’m Samyu (she/her). I’m a writer, organizer, and PhD student based in Los Angeles, CA. I specialize in the study of Race, Ethnicity, and Politics and Quantitative Methods in the Department of Political Science at the University of California, Los Angeles. My dissertation focuses on interventions to mitigate the impacts of anti-abortion direct action, such as clinic escorting and clinic defense, and the intervention preferences of subgroups of abortion seekers that are multiply marginalized, such as women of color.
